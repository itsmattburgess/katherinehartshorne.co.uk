Katherine Hartshorne

Professional actor, singer and producer.

Katherine is a versatile character and comedy actress. She has a vast amount of experience in theatre, film, television and voice acting. Some of her many talents include, comedy improv, horse riding, intermediate award in stage combat with A.P.C. and she is a lyrical mezzo-soprano with a range G3 to F5. With an understanding that an actor must always be improving and learning, Katherine continues to develop new skills.

Some of her previous credits include the lead in feature film, Dorcha (2019) and children series, Big Wheel (2019). For every production she brings her bright and vibrant personality along with an open mind. Katherine co-founded LoveHart Prodcitions in 2016.
